# Requirements

**Stream Nodes**

* **CPU**: 16 cores
* **Memory**: 32GB or more
* **Public IP**: Required
* **Bandwidth**: 100Mbps
* **Disk Capacity**: More than 1TB

**Storage Nodes**

* **CPU**: 16 cores
* **Memory**: 32GB or more
* **Disk Capacity**: More than 1TB

**Validator Nodes**

* **CPU**: 8 cores
* **Memory**: 16GB or more
* **Disk Capacity**: 1TB SSD
